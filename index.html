About Julia

Julia has spent years working alongside hairstylists with over 30 years of combined experience, learning to work with every type and texture of hair. Her friend helped her master the art of blow drying — especially for curly hair. Through that experience, Julia discovered her love for curls: how to celebrate them, enhance them, and tame them when needed.

Living in Florida brought new opportunities and challenges. Julia took it upon herself to keep learning and growing, evolving into a confident, creative, and compassionate hairstylist and entrepreneur. If you’ve ever struggled to find someone who truly understands curly hair, Julia is your stylist. She does a beautiful job with all hair types, and is growing her community of followers quickly!

The Hobe Sound community has been a beautiful home for her blossoming business, and she welcomes every new client with warmth, care, and expertise — no matter your hair type or style.
